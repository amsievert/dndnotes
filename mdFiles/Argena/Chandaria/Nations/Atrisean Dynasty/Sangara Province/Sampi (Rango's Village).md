# Overview
Sampi is a small village northwest of [[Sangara]] in the [[Atrisean Dynasty]]. It is almost exclusively inhabited by Lizardfolk, whose cold-blooded bodies enjoy the thick, swampy heat of this area. The population is small, no more than a few hundred, but it is rich in history and natural significance.

The village is very secluded from the rest of the world beyond the swamp. Many of the Lizardfolk inhabitants know nothing of the rest of [[Chandaria]]. The little that they do know comes mostly from missionaries that come from Sella Alora on behalf of the God King. These missionaries never stay for long however, as the Lizardfolk are a stubborn people and always prove incredibly devout to their ancient beliefs.

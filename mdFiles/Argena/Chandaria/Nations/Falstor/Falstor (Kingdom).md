# Overview
The Kingdom of Falstor is situated in the Southeast of [[Chandaria]], bordered by [[Rodigia|The Kingdom of Rodigia]] and the Howling Peaks to the north, and bordered on every other side by the waters of the Azure Expanse. The Kingdom's lands are dominated by fields and forests, however there is a thick swamp to the south.
## Government
Falstor is a monarchy, ruled by a King, who is selected from one of the capital city's [[Falstor (City)#The Royal Houses of Falstor|Royal Houses]], and is said to be divinely anointed by [[Pholtus]], the Herald of Light. The current king is [[King Ophal]]. He is a just and charismatic king who is generally well-liked.

The king maintains control over his kingdom through the Kingsguard. The Guard are a centralized police force, not trained for open military combat, but well-adapted for maintaining peace in the Kingdom's cities.

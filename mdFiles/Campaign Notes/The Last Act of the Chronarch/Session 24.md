Current Day: 25th of Calo, 2506 PS

The attack on the village begins. The invading force consists of a buffed Cinder Hulk (BGG) that has 250 hit points, 2 fire elementals, and an azer pyromancer (MM25, flavored as a ranged fire elemental). The villagers splash water on the elementals to fight back. Idu joins the fight but is quickly taken down by the Cinder Hulk. Sharvashk (Rango's son) also appears during the fight trying to defend a female and a few children who were out during the festival.

After the fight, Barack tells Rango that he believes he must undergo the Profane Ritual in order to avenge those who lost their lives in the attack. By undergoing the ritual now, he can bind himself to the souls of those who died and by killing this "Lord of Smolder and Ash", he can release their souls at the cost of his own being left out of the Beastlands. Mellok can facilitate this ritual.

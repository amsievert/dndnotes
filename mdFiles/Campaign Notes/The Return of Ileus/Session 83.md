# Crone's Cross
The Crone's Cross military camp is about 10 miles inland from Traskip Bay. The camp has a hardened earth wall around it with guard towers and only a few gates. The area outside the walls is a no man's land with symbol spells littered throughout. The few roads that do lead into the camp are gated and guarded with Dispel Magic devices, made by MoonCorp. The skies are patrolled by Griffin riders.

The general of the army is General Blaith Smil, the same general from the Rodigian Campaign. The quartermaster is Tully Rowe. The head mage is Dormar Taloval, who answers directly to Ileus.

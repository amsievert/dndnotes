Current Day: 26th of Hemlia, 2519 PS

Back in Falstor after the final war council, the party is invited to a play called "The Heroes of Holfirth" performed by the Falstor Player's Company at the Royal Theater. 

After the play, Clegane Jr. arrives at Alystri manor, ready for battle. Godfrey is also at the manor for one last feast. Stefan is awake and is also at the feast. He is adamant about going into battle with the Alystri soldiers.

The next morning, the Falstor army leaves, marching North through the desert, headed for the Fallen Daggers. It takes 14.5 days to reach the Kha'anj, where the full coalition army assembles. From there, they march the final 3 days to the plains just south of the Fallen Daggers. Just outside the mountain pass, the glow of the Larenthian camp looms large on the horizon.

The Ordning is the social structure that governs Giant society. It is a strict social hierarchy that organizes all Giants, even within the different types of Giantkind. The Ordning is said to derive from the birth order of Annam's children, at least among the base species of Giants.

The Ordning:
- Storm Giants
- Cloud Giants
- Fire Giants
- Frost Giants
- Stone Giants
- Fomorians
- Hill Giants
- Ettins
- Ogres

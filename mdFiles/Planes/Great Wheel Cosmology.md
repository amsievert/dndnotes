The Great Wheel is a theoretical arrangement of all known planes of existence to assist mortals in conceptualizing the universe. During the [[An Overall History Of Chandaria#The Arcane Resurgence|Arcane Resurgence]], the [[The Kainomantic Principles|Kainomancers]] began uncovering the truths of the [[The Great Dissension|Great Dissension]] and were attempting to map out all of the different forces and beings that took part in the conflict. The found it useful to arrange the planes of existence into a map-like representation that related alignment similarity with geographic distance.
